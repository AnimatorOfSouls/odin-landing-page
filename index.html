Hikaru No Go

A webpage about the anime "Hikaru No Go", which discusses a young boy called Hikaru who meets a Go obsessed ghost from 1000 years ago, and their journey towards being a professional go player.

Major Characters

Shindou Hikaru
The main character of the show. He learns to play Go and becomes a professional go player.

Fujiwara no Sai
A 1000 year old ghost from the Heian era. Serves as Hikaru's mentor in Go and dreams of playing the divine move.

Touya Akira
Hikaru's rival, where they drive each other to greater heights in go as they chase the hand of god.
